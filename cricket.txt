Cricket is a popular sport enjoyed by millions across the globe. It originated in England and is now popular in many countries, especially those in the Commonwealth. The game is played between two teams of eleven players each. There are various formats of the game including Test matches, One-Day Internationals, and Twenty20 Internationals. The objective of the game is to score more runs than the opposing team. Cricket is not just a game, but a symbol of unity and camaraderie. The sport has produced many legends like Sachin Tendulkar, Sir Don Bradman, and Sir Vivian Richards. The game is governed by the International Cricket Council. Cricket teaches us discipline, patience and the spirit of sportsmanship. The thrill and excitement of cricket make it a truly global sport.
