India, officially known as the Republic of India, is the seventh largest country by land mass in the world. It is located in South Asia and is bordered by Pakistan to the northwest, China and Nepal to the north, Bhutan to the northeast, and Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east. India is a democratic republic consisting of 28 states and 7 union territories. It has a population of over 1.3 billion people, making it the second most populous country in the world. India's economy is the world's fifth-largest by nominal GDP. India is known for its rich and diverse culture, with over 2,000 distinct ethnic groups and more than 1,600 spoken languages. The country is also home to a variety of wildlife in a range of protected habitats. India has made significant strides in various domains of science and technology over the years. It has also been the birthplace of several significant scientific and mathematical concepts. From the ancient times, India has been the land of sages, saints and various renowned scholars. Numerous individuals from this country have made their mark in various domains globally with their prowess. From science to arts, Indian scholars have left no stone unturned with their extraordinary achievements.
